Week 2 Lecture Notes

  • All Racket programs are built out of parentheses-enclosed expressions called S-expressions. These S-expressions are “untyped” in the sense that they are just symbolic data. They can nest, nested S-expressions are akin to trees.

    • We will discuss S-expressions more significantly later on in the course.
